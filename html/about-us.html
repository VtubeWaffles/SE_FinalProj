About Us

De Kuwadro Visuals

De Kuwadro Visuals is a media brand that offers photograpy and videography services to clients. It is a start-up business owned by Carl Matulina, a third year BSIT student of WVSU-CICT and was established last November 23, 2022.

The small business aims to provide a service for people to treasure every moment through photo and video documentation. The owner covers various types of events including but not necessarily limited to special moments such as weddings, debuts, birthdays, pageants, baptisms, and corporal events.
